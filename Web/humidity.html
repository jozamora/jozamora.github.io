Humidity




Analyzing the latitude versus humidity, it is harder to distinguish the correlation these two have. It appears as though humidity isn't directly related to latitude. As we can see on the scatter plot, there seems to be humidity in cities regardless of the latitude. It seems as though humidity is determined by other factors that are not directly related to latitude.
