Max Temperature




In regards to latitude vs temperature it seems like there is a direct correlation between the two. By looking at Figure 1 we can see that the closer in latitude to the equator the higher the max temperature for those cities. As we move away from the equator in both directions we can see there is a drop off in max temperature for the cities.
One thing to keep is the reason for the northern latitudes having more data points is because the Earth's landmass is concentrated above the equator leading to more northern cities.
